Movie Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people's dreams. He is given a chance to erase his criminal past by planting an idea into someone's subconscious, blurring the line between dreams and reality.

Main Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Elliot Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito. Each actor brings depth to the complex dream-world narrative.
