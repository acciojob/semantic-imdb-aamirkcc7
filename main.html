Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by performing the impossible task of planting an idea into someone's mind—a process known as inception.

Main Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio stars as Dom Cobb, alongside Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe. Each actor brings depth to the complex, layered story.
